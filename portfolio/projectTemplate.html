doodle war

A project carried out as part of the GrosJoueurs competition at UQAT from fall 2019 to winter 2020. Doodle War is an interactive and fun visual experience in which participants express their creativity through art. Once they draw something, they can see their creations come to life, move around, and combat others in an arena projected onto a wall. As the interface designer, I designed both the UX and UI, the logo, and took care of gathering visual assets.

The team behind this project included Olivier Coupal (VFX), Laurent Duthoy (integration), Alexandra Lamarre (AI programming), Nathan Lamothe (programming), Andric Roy-Doyon (programming), and Vincent Thoer-Fabre (systems design). The project was presented at the Nuit Blanche 2020 on February 29.
