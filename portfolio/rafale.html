rafale

A project carried out as part of the Experience Design course at Collège Montpetit in winter 2018, Rafale is the prototype of a video game app in which players play a variety of micro-games in order to to score as many points as possible, similar to games in the WarioWare series. As the lead UI designer, I created the logo, the visual identity, as well as all the interfaces and animations presented here.

The project was made with Arnaud Bérubé-Duval and Nicolas Berthiaume, who worked on the wireframes and the logo animation (not shown here.)
